<h1 class="page-title">Welcome to the deep end</h1>
<p class="subtitle">the riptide of the abyss</p>

This site is devoted to small fragments curated from actual games played and other musings about the world of crafting creative stories with dice and paper. 

![Harry Clark - Tales of Mystery and Imagination](/images/descent-into-the-maelstrom.jpg "The boat appeared to be hanging, as if by magic, ... upon the interior surface of a funnel.")

There will be small glimpses of other fictions too, adjacent stories to the ones derived from the whirlpool of the infinite game.
