## Cargo list

- A jagged, curved relic, maybe some alien tooth? 
- An empty suit of polished armor (space suit?)
- A grim map, fused in some kind of reflector dish (a star chart?) (**_Note_**_: this item was later used to defend against the Deep Hunter, a Cursed Vessel, that had caught up to me early in my sailing._)
- Some brick or keystone from an old fortification, marked with cryptic, runic letters, chiseled in the cold, stone-like material (remnant of an old bio-home?) 
- An ornate compass, made of brass and diamonds, but completely broken, de-magnetized.
- An old and ragged flag depicting a shoreline and a horizon, mostly in tatters.

![La guerre des mondes - la delgue ici](/images/pdia-34130069-1049-45a5-9e9a-0fddde108dd5%20%281%29.jpg)

I learned later that the flood had wiped out a valley and I alone was responsible for dredging up what was left of a past consumed by water. There were many hints of drowned, whale-like gods in the oceanic valley. Perhaps I was sent to retain what was left of them so they would not be forgotten.
