About

Welcome to Twelve-Sided Leviathan, a blog dedicated to tabletop roleplaying game design, dungeon crawling, and exploration-based gameplay.

What This Blog Is About

This blog focuses on:

  • Dungeon Design: Creating dungeons that support exploration and meaningful player choices
  • Game Mechanics: Understanding how classic D&D mechanics work and why they matter
  • OSR Principles: Old School Renaissance design philosophy and play styles
  • Adventure Design: Writing adventures that encourage player agency and exploration

About the Author

I'm a TTRPG enthusiast who has been playing and designing games for many years. I'm particularly interested in how game mechanics support different play styles and how to create adventures that give players meaningful choices.

Contact

You can find me on various RPG forums and communities. Feel free to reach out if you have questions or want to discuss game design!
